Number Ninja Challenge
Welcome to Number Ninja - The Ultimate Number Guessing Game! Get ready to put your ninja-like intuition to the test in this thrilling number guessing challenge! Choose your path and embark on an exciting adventure with two distinct modes: Easy and Difficult.

Easy Mode: In Easy mode, you'll face the challenge of guessing a secret three-digit number generated by the computer. But don't worry, you have 5 chances to crack the code! The game will provide you with 2 hints upfront to get you started. And if you stumble along the way, fear not, as you'll receive an additional hint after every wrong guess. Can you use your wit and strategy to guess the number within the given chances? Find out and claim victory!

Difficult Mode: For the brave souls seeking a true test of their ninja skills, Difficult mode is the ultimate challenge. Here, you'll have only 3 chances to unveil the elusive three-digit number. Before you begin, the game will grant you one valuable hint to set you on your path. With each incorrect guess, you'll receive a hint to guide your next move. Brace yourself for the heart-pounding excitement and take on this epic challenge of Number Ninja. Think you have what it takes to master the art of Number Ninja? Choose your mode wisely, and let the quest begin! Good luck, fearless warrior, and may your ninja instincts guide you to victory!
